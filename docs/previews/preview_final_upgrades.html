The Final Upgrades

Fusing "Neon Build" & "Tracing Laser" with the 80s Masters
Tracing Laser × Patrick Nagel
The Razor Laser
Nagel's sharp minimalism applied to the Laser. Clean, hard-edged geometric lasers slice through a dark slate track, leaving precision green and purple cuts. No glowing—just pure sharpness.
Tracing Laser × Peter Saville
The Data Trace
Saville's pulsing data lines applied to the Laser. Instead of one thick line, multiple staggered data packets race independently down parallel tracks like fiber optic cables.
Neon Build × Keith Haring
The Action Build
Haring's pop-art energy applied to the Neon Build. The Z draws itself quickly, and the moment it finishes, thick purple action lines violently vibrate out of its corners to signify it's "live", before resetting.
Neon Build × Hajime Sorayama
The Chrome & Flare Build
Sorayama's hyper-reflective chrome applied to the Neon Build. The Z rapidly draws itself in thick metal. Upon completion, a massive green and purple lens flare glides along the diagonal.
